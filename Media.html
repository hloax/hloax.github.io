Seizing opportunities at NAMPO 2024 to fuel agricultural growth in the Lejweleputswa District and beyond. 

Lejwe Le Putswa Development Agency held their two-day Strategic Planning Session in Parys where employees could have a say on how to improve and make LDA known by community and companies and how they could benefit. All this was made possible by Honorable LDM Mayor (Mrs. Ntakumbana), Honorable LDA Board Chairman (Mr. Besani), Board Members and our LDA CEO (Mr. L Motsamai). And I would also like to thank our key speakers Nedbank, SEFA, SETA, NEF, IDC, DESTEA, COGTA and all local municipalities from Lejweleputswa District
